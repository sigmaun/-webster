Professor Webster is the Bob Herberger Arizona Heritage Chair in supply chain management at the W. P. Carey School of Business at Arizona State University. His research focuses primarily on problems related to pricing and inventory, and on challenges that arise in agriculture and nonprofit settings. He has worked in industry in the areas of consulting and finance. He teaches graduate classes in business analytics and supply chain management.
